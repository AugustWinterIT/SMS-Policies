SMS Notification Terms and Conditions

Internal Employee Notification Program

These Terms and Conditions govern the internal SMS notification program operated by August Winter & Sons, Inc. for employee use only.

Program Description

August Winter & Sons, Inc. sends internal SMS notifications to employees for business operations purposes. Messages may include, but are not limited to, visitor arrival alerts, internal announcements, operational updates, and company news.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on business activity and operational needs. Some notifications are triggered by specific events (such as a visitor checking in), while others may be sent as needed for company announcements or updates.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. August Winter & Sons, Inc. does not charge employees for receiving these notifications.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out of SMS notifications at any time. For assistance, reply HELP or contact IT directly.

Support Contact

For questions or assistance regarding this SMS program, contact the IT department at it@augustwinter.com.

Privacy

Employee cell phone numbers are used solely for internal business notification purposes and are never shared with third parties. For full details, see our SMS Notification Privacy Policy.
