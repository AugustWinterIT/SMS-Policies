SMS Notification Privacy Policy

Internal Employee Notification Program

This Privacy Policy describes how August Winter & Sons, Inc. collects, uses, and protects employee mobile phone numbers in connection with our internal SMS notification program.

Data Collected

August Winter & Sons, Inc. collects employee mobile phone numbers for the purpose of delivering internal SMS notifications related to business operations, including but not limited to visitor arrival alerts, internal announcements, operational updates, and company news.

How It Is Used

Phone numbers are used exclusively to send SMS notifications for internal business purposes. Numbers are not used for marketing, promotional communications, or any purpose unrelated to legitimate internal business operations.

How Consent Is Obtained

New employees provide their mobile phone number to the HR department during the onboarding process and are informed that their number will be used to receive internal SMS notifications for business operations purposes. Existing employees will be notified via email and company newsletter of the company's intention to communicate via text at times.

Third-Party Sharing

Employee phone numbers are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for any purpose. SMS messages are transmitted via Twilio, our messaging service provider, solely for the purpose of message delivery. Twilio does not retain employee data beyond what is necessary for transmission.

Opt-Out

Employees may opt out of SMS notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message. To opt back in or update contact information, reach out to IT at it@augustwinter.com.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns regarding this program, contact the IT department at it@augustwinter.com.
